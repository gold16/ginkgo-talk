# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Be respectful in discussions and reviews
- Focus on technical facts and collaboration
- Accept feedback gracefully

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discrimination
- Trolling, insulting, or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and comments that violate this code.

Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.
